About Ravi Milk Shop

Established in 2001, Ravi Milk Shop is a family-owned dairy farm committed to providing fresh, pure and nutritious dairy products to our community. What started as a small operation has now grown to serve the entire Rasipuram area.

We believe in traditional farming methods combined with modern hygiene practices to deliver the healthiest dairy products. Our animals are grass-fed, free-range and treated with care and respect. We never use hormones or antibiotics to increase milk production.
